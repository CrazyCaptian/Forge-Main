White Paper

What is Forge?

Forge is the first tokenized contract on the Polygon blockchain to be one hundred percent community owned, decentralized, fair, trustless, and ownerless. Forge uses a POW system that distributes rewards, and enables our new auctioning feature.

Forge Token Distribution

21,000,000 distributed via Proof of Work aka Mining (50%)
10,500,000 distributed via Auctions (25%)
10,500,000 distributed via Stakings (25%)

Forge Auctions

25% of Forges' token supply is distributed by the Forge Auctions contract.
The amount of Forge mined by Proof-of-Work miners will determine the auction length.  Averages to 72 hour auctions.
Each auction will sell 8,192 Forge Tokens split to all bidders evenly depending on how much 0xBitcoin they bid for the auction.
Your 0xBTC Deposited into Auction / Total 0xBTC for Auction * 8,192 Forge = Your Forge Tokens you will recieve at that auction.
Auctions contract has a way to bid on future auctions and multiple auctions at once! Use the Advanced Auctions in dAPP.
This allows users to bid for multiple auctions without needing to send a transaction every 3 days!  
Allowing decentralized dollar cost averaging of Forge! Users will be able to spread buys over multiple auctions.
Users have a way to claim multiple auctions at once with a press of the button and in one small transaction.
Empowering users a way to fairly bid on every auction without needing to be present at the time of the auction, all while saving transaction fees!
100% decentralized, trustless, and fair based on amount of 0xBitcoin spent at that auction.

Liquidity Mining


25% of the Forge Token Supply is distributed by the Liquidity Provider Staking Contract
The speed at which Stakers are rewarded will be deteremined by Proof-of-Work miners.
Average 3,0000 Forge daily for the first era.
66% of 0xBitcoin collected by Forge Auctions contract will be rewarded to stakers.
The rewards are split fairly and disitributed continously to all Stakers.  
We are staking ONLY Quickswap Forge/0xBitcoin Liquidiy Pool tokens.
This will fairly and decentrally distribute tokens to our Liquidity Providers, helping defeat Impermanent Loss(IL) often associated with it.

Distribution of 0xBitcoin Collected by Forge Auction Contract

67% of the 0xBTC raised from the Auctions contract goes directly to Miners via Proof-of-Work
33% of the 0xBTC raised from the Auctions contract goes back to Liquidiy Providers via the Staking Contract!

POW Mining

50% of the Forge Token Supply is distributed to Proof-of-Work Miners.  They solve a complicated hash that rewards them Forge tokens. 
Every solve generatres a new complicated hash for the next miner to solve.  All done on chain, trustless, and ownerless. 100% decentralized.
Along with the Forge Tokens, miners will also recieve 0xBitcoin for every solve.  
33% of 0xBitcoin collected by Forge Auctions contract will be rewarded to miners.
Miners will make 0xBTC profit along with their Forge Tokens daily, helping elivate transaction costs, electral costs, and even hardware costs!  
Mining reward is based on length between blocks, now miners are rewarded for lowering their hashrate instead of increasing it!
Allowing Forge to be fully mined forever while promoting a greener token distribution!

Why should I mine for Forge?

Along with the Forge Tokens and the 0xBitcoin Tokens, miners will have the ability to mine up to 50 other Cryptocurrencies!
Any ERC20 sent to the Forge Contract will become instantly mineable! With most of the supply being distributed over 1 year.
This allows any token to become mineable, without having to run their own proof-of-work system.
Our system allows for basically any token to be mined, since miners pay nothing!
We expect to see Fractionalized NFTs avaialble for mining
No dev fee or any sort of fee is taken. All Cryptocurrencies are distributed 100% to the Forge community.
