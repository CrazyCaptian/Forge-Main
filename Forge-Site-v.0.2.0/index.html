  Auction

Auction your tokens for FORGE here

A way to securely auction Forge. Auctions end every three days.
Forge Auctions contract only accepts 0xBitcoin Tokens to bid during auctions of Forge Tokens.
After auction ends the Forge auctioned divides 8,192 Forge by how much 0xBitcoin user sent. 
Each participant will receive their share of Forge after auction ends.
The amount of Forge you recieve at action is as follows
8,192 Forge * Amount of 0xBitcoin YOU bid on the current auction / Total Amount of 0xBitcoin bid at the current auction
    ex 1: If you bid 1 0xBitcoin and I bid 3 0xBitcoin and the auction ends.
    ex 1: You will recieve (8,192 Forge) * 1 0xBTC / 4 0xBTC = 2,048 Forge
    ex 1: I will recieve (8,192 Forge) * 3 0xBTC / 4 0xBTC = 6,144 Forge
At token launch each auction has 8,192 claimable Forge and will halve (to 4,096 Forge) after 600 auctions (4 years).

 Stake

Stake Here


Holders of Forge/0xBitcoin liquidity tokens will be rewarded in Forge & 0xBitcoin tokens.
Staking uses ONLY the Forge/0xBitcoin Liquidity Pool Tokens for Quickswap.
By staking Quickswap Forge/0xBitcoin liquidity tokens it allows Liquidity Providers(LPers) a way to fight impermanent loss(IL).
At token launch rewards are ~3,000 tokens a day, split among all depositors in the staking contract.
The staking contract will distribute 0xBitcoin tokens, Forge, Polygon and up to two other coins!
Stakers also receive 33% of all 0xBitcoin collected by the Forge Auctions contract.
LPers will still recieve 0.25% of all trades that uses the pool, while staked!
We aim to be the largest Forge and 0xBitcoin pool on Polygon, getting the most trades routed through us!

Mine

Download Our Miner and Get Started

A way to securely mint Forge tokens using our safe Proof of Work system and contracts.
Machines connected to the contract solve complicated hashes with an answer unique to their ethereum address for the reward
Each successful solve takes around 60 minutes.  Rewards are based on time between blocks.
Receive a mining reward of up to 50+ different cryptocurrencies and Forge in our mining lottery!
Mining rate controls auction rate, and stake rate, the more tokens mined the more tokens for sale/rewarded.
Miners could also be rewarded with wrapped NFTs or other valuable items that earn rewards in 12 hour intervals.
Mining reward is based on length of the solve.  Longer solves get larger rewards.  Skewed to promote hashrate loss rather than gain!
    ex. 1: Mining a block in 60 minutes will recieve 150 Forge
    ex. 2: Mining a block in 120 minutes will recieve 600 Forge
    ex. 3: Mining a block in 1 minute will recieve ~3 Forge
This reward will half every every ~4 years.
Miners also receive 66% of all 0xBitcoin collected by the Forge Auctions contract.
