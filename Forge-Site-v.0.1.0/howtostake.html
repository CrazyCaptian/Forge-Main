How Do I Auction?

Participate in Auctions

A MetaMask Account with polygon bridged 0xBitcoin is required to auction.

Getting Forge and 0xBitcoin Tokens

To get the Forge/0xBitcoin Qucikswap LP Token you need 0xBitcoin and Forge in equal parts, 1:1 ratio in price.  
You get Forge Tokens via bidding in Auctions on the DAPP or by swapping with Quickswap @ (coming soon)
You buy 0xBitcoin Tokens via Quickswap (coming soon)

Getting Forge/0xBitcoin Quickswap Liquidiy Pool Tokens

To stake we need to get the Forge/0xBitcoin Liquidity Token from Quickswap.
Go to Quickswap and click Pool
Add the Forge Contract Address (Address HERE)
Add the 0xBitcoin Contract Address (Addresses HERE)
Choose the amount you wish to LP.
Supply the tokens and recieve the Forge/0xBitcoin LP token

Depositing the Forge/0xBitcoin LP Tokens into the Staking Contract

To stake we use the Staking Contract @ address here.
To stake you simply supply Forge/0xBitcoin LP Tokens into the DAPP and receive your fair share of rewards.
Click here to go to the DAPP.
On the DAPP Page click Staking Tab at the top.
Deposits require two transactions, Approve and Deposit.
Approve the Staking contract to Deposit your LP Tokens in Metamask.
Then approve the Deposit Transaction in Metamask.

Reverse these steps for withdrawing from contracts.

How do rewards get paid to stakers?

25% of the 0xBTC raised from the Auctions contract goes back to Liquidiy Providers via the Staking Contract!
No dev fee or any sort of fee is taken. All Cryptocurrencies are distributed 100% to the Forge community.
