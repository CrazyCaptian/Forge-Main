Auction

Auction your tokens for FORGE here

A way to securely auction Forge. Auctions end every three days.
Our contract only accepts 0xBitcoin Tokens to bid during Auction of Forge Tokens.
After auction ends the reward divides by how much 0xBitcoin user sent. 
Each participant will fairly receive their share of claimable Forge at auction end.
At token launch Forge auction will provide 8,192 claimable Forge and will half every 600 auctions (estimated 4-5 years).

Stake

Stake Here

Holders of 0xBitcoin/Forge liquidity token will be rewarded in shiny tokens.
By staking liquidity tokens users allow the contract to grow and get larger as time goes on.
At token launch rewards are ~3,000 tokens a day. Split among all depositors in the staking contract.
The contract will distribute 0xBitcoin tokens, Forge, Polygon and possibly two other coins!
LP stakers also receive 33% of all 0xBitcoin minted by the Auction contract

Mine

Download Our Miner and Get Started

A way to securely mint Forge tokens using our safe POW system and contracts.
Machines connected to the contract solve complicated hashes with an answer unique to their ethereum address for the reward
Each successful solve takes around 30 minutes, each solve rewards 150 Forge tokens. This reward will half every every ~4 years.
Receive a mining reward of up to 50+ different cryptocurrencies and Forge in our mining lottery!
Mining rate controls auction rate, and stake rate, the more tokens mined the more tokens for sale/rewarded.
Miners could also be rewarded with wrapped NFTs or other valuable items that earn rewards in 12 hour intervals.
Miners receive 66% of all 0xBitcoin made by the Auction contract.
